# Superstore Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes a retail dataset using SQL and Power BI in order to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and profitability drivers. The objective is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights.

---

## Tools

* MySQL for data exploration and aggregation
* Power BI for data visualization
* Excel for initial data cleaning

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains transactional information including orders, customers, products, sales, profit, discount, and regional data.

---

## Analysis

The analysis is divided into four main areas:

### Executive Overview

Provides a general view of business performance, including total revenue, profit, orders, customers, and profit margin. It also highlights the relationship between discount levels and profitability.

### Customer Analysis

Focuses on identifying high-value and unprofitable customers. It includes segmentation analysis and comparison of sales and profit across customer groups.

### Sales Analysis

Examines sales performance across time, categories, regions, and segments. It also identifies the products that contribute the most to revenue.

### Profitability Analysis

Explores profit distribution and identifies factors affecting profitability. Special attention is given to discount levels and loss-generating sub-categories.

---

## Key Findings

* Revenue is concentrated in specific categories and regions
* The Consumer segment represents the largest share of sales
* High discount levels are associated with reduced profitability
* Some sub-categories consistently generate losses
* Profitability varies significantly across segments and regions
